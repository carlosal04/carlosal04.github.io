Our Company

Basaltos y Agregados S.A. de C.V., is a company whose objective is, the direction and execution of projects in the field of Construction and Engineering such as Paving, Bridges, Urbanization, Building, Installations in the Public and Private Sector. For this we have great highly qualified personnel and experts in the field to satisfy all the needs of our clients.

Vision

Contribute to the strengthening of the South-Southeast in its high competitiveness index and by quality reflected in the works and projects we undertake, satisfactorily meeting the goals and objectives of partners and collaborators, being at the forefront, using state-of-the-art technology, with a great respect for the environment.

Mission

Give attention to specific construction needs in the area, with the highest standards quality, supported by a philosophy formed by institutional values, thereby generating well-being and development of all collaborators, as well as; guarantee our permanence and leadership in the market.
